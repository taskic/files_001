ABOUT

On this world environment day we students of Alan Feldman Public School decided to come up with a new initiative which gives school children an opportunity to showcase their talent, skill & abililty.

It is an online platform which opens the door to various competitions to say essay writing, photography, quiz, shortfilm etc. concentrating to bring about that creativity and innovation in their thought process.

The title of this initiative is TASK-IC. The reason behind such a title is after understanding each and everyone of us.

To further say TASK-IC represents:


-> T - Talent

-> A - Ability

-> S - Skill

-> K - Knowledge


-> I - Innovation

-> C - Creativity


"By creating this platform we got an wonderful opportunity to put up our own capability and mastery in it."
